Check out our Instagram page to stay up to date with club life. But most importantly you'll be able to try and solve a daily chess puzzles we post in our story. Let's all keep ourselves trained.

Online Tournaments

We orginize online tournaments mainly on lichess, open to everyone, join our team on lichess and/or chess.com and be part of the crew.

OTB Tournaments

Over the board tournaments are the best way to meet new friends and get the best out of the club experience. They're organized whenever the spaces are available, watch out for updated in our telegram group so you'll make sure not to miss them.

Seminars

If you're intrested in anyway about the engeeniring and scientific side of chess you might like to participate in our seminars, which are held by the University's professors from different faculties.
